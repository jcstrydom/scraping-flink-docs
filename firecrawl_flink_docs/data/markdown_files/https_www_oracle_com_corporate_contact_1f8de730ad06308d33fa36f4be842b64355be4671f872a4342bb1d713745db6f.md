#### Use the following information to get technical help

- **Customers with active support contracts** should access the [My Oracle Support](https://support.oracle.com/portal/) portal to find answers to their technical questions in the Oracle Support knowledgebase, to participate in the [My Oracle Support Community](https://community.oracle.com/mosc/), or to submit and update service requests. Please refer to the [Oracle Support Contacts Global Directory](https://www.oracle.com/support/contact.html) for information on renewing Premier Support, regional support sales contacts, and support policies.
- **Cloud subscription customers** should go to the [Oracle Cloud Portal](https://www.oracle.com/cloud/sign-in.html) to access Getting Started guides for their cloud products, contact their cloud customer success manager, ask operational questions, or join a [discussion forum](https://community.oracle.com/customerconnect/).
- **Customers with products from businesses acquired by Oracle** should go to the [Acquired Product Support Directory](https://www.oracle.com/support/contact.html#acquired) for their support needs.
