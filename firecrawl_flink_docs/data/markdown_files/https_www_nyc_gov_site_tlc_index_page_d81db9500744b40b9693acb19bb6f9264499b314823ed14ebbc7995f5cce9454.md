## [Connect to the TLC Owner/Driver Resource Center](https://portal.driverresourcecenter.tlc.nyc.gov/)

Latest News

The TLC Owner/Driver Resource Center is a space where Licensees may seek free financial counseling, legal services and other resources, including the Medallion Relief Program and Loan Guaranty Program (MRP+) which provide debt relief for those with five or fewer medallions. These programs provide free legal representation to help medallion owners negotiate with lenders to reduce loan balances and lower monthly payments.

## What can we help you find?

- [Apply for a license](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/drivers/get-a-tlc-drivers-license.page) TLC Driver, Base, and Vehicle licenses
- [Lost property](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/passengers/report-lost-property.page) File a report for missing property online or over the phone
- [Take a drug test](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/drivers/drug-testing-requirements.page) Understand requirements and find a testing site near you
- [Driver education](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/drivers/driver-education.page) Education requirements before and during licensure
- [Check the status of a license](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/tlc-online/tlc-up.page) The TLCUP portal for checking license and application status
- [Make a licensing appointment](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/tlc-online/licensing-standards-appointment-scheduling.page) Visit our licensing division in-person
- [Taxi fares](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/passengers/taxi-fare.page) All metered fares for riding in a yellow or green taxi
- [Update your records or pay a fine](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/tlc-online/lars.page) The LARS portal to update your address and contact information and pay fines
- [Get an accessible ride](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/passengers/find-an-accessible-ride.page) Request a wheelchair accessible vehicle
- [Pass your vehicle inspection/ Prepare for your vehicle](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/vehicles/vehicle-inspections.page) Review inspection requirements
- [TLC data](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/about/data-and-research.page) Read reports and research data
- [Vehicle, Plates, and Base Transfer](https://www.nyc.gov/site/tlc/vehicles/vehicle-transfer.page) Requirements and applications
